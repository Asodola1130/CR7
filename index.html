Cristiano Ronaldo

Biography

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro, born 5 February 1985, is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and captains the Portugal national team. Wikipedia

Achievements

A famous quote: "I see myself as the best footballer in the world. If you don't believe you are the best, then you will never achieve all that you are capable of."

Legacy

Ronaldo's influence on football is unparalleled, with numerous records and an inspirational journey from a small island in Portugal to global stardom.
